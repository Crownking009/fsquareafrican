Service Questions

What you may want to know before you order or book

Do you cater all kinds of events?

Yes. We cater birthdays, weddings, naming ceremonies, church gatherings, office events, family parties, memorials, and other private functions where Nigerian food is needed.

How early should I book catering?

For bigger events, earlier is always better so we can plan portions, timing, and logistics properly. Smaller tray orders and routine pickups can often be arranged with shorter notice depending on the day.

Can I request custom combinations and tray sizes?

Yes. We can help you combine rice dishes, soups, swallows, proteins, local drinks, and sides in portions that fit your guest count and style of service.

Do you deliver large orders and event catering?

We support both collection and delivery arrangements. Delivery timing for large orders is planned with you in advance so meals arrive close to service time.

Can you handle office lunches and repeat group orders?

Absolutely. We can support recurring group meals, staff lunches, and event-day refresh orders with practical menu planning and clear communication.

Need a catering quote?

Send your event date, estimated guest count, preferred dishes, and whether you need pickup or delivery. We will help you shape a simple, practical order plan.
