Our Privacy Policy

F Square African Food respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the information you share when you browse our website, contact us, make enquiries, or place food orders.

How We Collect and Use Information

  • 01.We may collect your name, phone number, email address, delivery details, order details, and messages you send through our website forms.

  • 02.We use this information to process orders, respond to enquiries, arrange delivery or catering, improve our service, and keep accurate business records.

  • 03.We do not sell your personal information. We only share details when needed to complete your order, meet legal requirements, or protect our website and business.

  • 04.We take reasonable steps to keep customer information secure, but no website or internet transmission can be guaranteed to be completely risk free.

  • 05.You may contact us to request an update, correction, or deletion of your personal information where applicable.

Additional Policy

Our website may use basic cookies or browser storage to support cart, wishlist, admin, and menu features. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

We may update this policy from time to time. The current version published on this page applies when you use the website.
